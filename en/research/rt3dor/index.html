Real-Time 3D Object Recognition

During my Bachelor's Thesis I was working on the development of a real-time 3D object recognition system optimized for its deployment on an embedded platform such as the NVIDIA Jetson TK1. The thesis was succesfully defended and graded with the highest mark (with honors). You can check it out using the following links:
